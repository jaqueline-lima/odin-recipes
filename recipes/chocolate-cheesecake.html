Chocolate Cheesecake

Description

A delicious chocolate cheesecake from the Youtube channel Nino's Home. No bake, no gelatin.

Ingredients

  • 14 Oreo cookies
  • 40g melted butter
  • 300g dark chocolate
  • Hot water
  • 300g cheesecake
  • 1tbsp vanilla extract
  • 40g sugar
  • 300g whipping cream

Steps

  1. Put the Oreo cookies in a plastic bag and crunch them
  2. Add the melted butter and mix well
  3. Put the mixture in a 15cm mold with a removable bottom and and press it hard
  4. Leave the mold in the refrigerator for 20 minutes
  5. Melt 200g of dark chocolate
  6. Put the creamcheese in a bowl and whisk it to make it softer
  7. Add the vanilla extract and sugar, then whisk it
  8. Add the melted chocolate to the mixture and whisk it again
  9. Whisk 200g of whipped cream until it's soft
  10. Add the whipping cream to the previous mixture and mix it lightly with the folding technique
  11. Put the mixture in the mold and refrigerate for 4 hours
  12. Put 100g os whipping cream in a pan and bring it to a soft boil
  13. Once it is boiling, put in a cup with 100g of dark chocolate and mix it to melt the chocolate
  14. Remove the cake from the mold and pour the whipping cream with chocolate on top of it
